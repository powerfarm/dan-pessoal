Yes, **NFC (Near Field Communication)** can trigger **Shortcuts (Atalhos)** on iOS and Android devices!

### **iOS (iPhone)**:

You can use NFC tags to trigger automations through the **Shortcuts app**.

1.  **Open the Shortcuts app.**
2.  Go to the **Automation** tab.
3.  Tap **Create Personal Automation**.
4.  Scroll down and select **NFC**.
5.  Scan your NFC tag by holding your phone near it.
6.  Name the NFC tag for identification.
7.  Add the action(s) you want (e.g., open an app, send a message, play music, etc.).
8.  Turn **off** "Ask Before Running" (optional, but useful for seamless activation).
9.  Save the automation.

When you scan the NFC tag with your iPhone, the associated shortcut will be triggered automatically.

### **Android**:

NFC tag triggering requires third-party apps or native tools like:

1.  **NFC Tools / Tasker**:
    *   Install the **NFC Tools** or **Tasker** app.
    *   Write actions or tasks to an NFC tag.
    *   When you tap the NFC tag with your phone, it will perform the task (e.g., changing settings, sending messages).
2.  Some Android devices (depending on the version and UI) support built-in **NFC automation via routines or profiles**.
